## Overview
Today TipJar.purchase()'s switch (Sources/Services/TipJar.swift ~102-109) silently breaks on .pending / .userCancelled and the catch block deliberately swallows errors. On an Ask-to-Buy (or SCA) account the spinner just stops with zero acknowledgment, and a transient StoreKit failure looks identical to a cancel. Nothing is lost (the Transaction.updates listener at ~line 42 finishes and records an eventually-approved purchase), so this is feedback-only polish: track a lightweight status on TipJar and render it as a quiet one-line note in AboutView's tip section.

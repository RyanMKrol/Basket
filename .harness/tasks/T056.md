## Overview

It runs forever, forcing a full list re-render every minute even when the Got-it section is empty and the tick changes nothing user-visible (the ticker exists to age recentlyGot items out against their 1-hour TTL and to keep time-of-day flourishes fresh; the `.animation(value: items.map(\.isChecked))` array at ~lines 100-103 is re-allocated on every tick-driven redraw - evidence of the churn, NOT something this task changes). Foreground-only app, so the energy win is modest; the redraw-churn reduction is the realer half.

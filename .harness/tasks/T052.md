## Overview
With Theme's fonts now scaling via relativeTo (prerequisite unit), the standing exclusion of the `.dynamicType` category in UITests/AccessibilityAuditTests.swift line 32, justified by a 'pixel fonts are fixed-size by design' comment that is no longer true, can be retired. The remaining fallout is every text element still built with a non-scaling font: there are ~22 `.system(size:)` usages across 10 files in Sources/ (AddBar, AboutView, EmptyStateView, ShoppingListView, QuantityEditor, ItemRow's emoji and icons, etc.), some of which the audit will flag, plus the risk that the card layouts overlap or clip at the largest accessibility sizes. This unit re-enables the category, fixes what it flags, and validates layout at large sizes, with an explicitly authorized cap if the layout genuinely breaks at the top of the range.

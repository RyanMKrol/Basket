# T002 — Fix ClearedCelebration dismiss animation curve

Date: 2026-07-04

## What I did

In `Sources/Views/ShoppingListView.swift`'s `celebrateCleared()`, changed the
deferred dismiss animation from `.easeIn(duration: 0.4)` to
`.easeOut(duration: 0.5)`. The old `.easeIn` curve stayed near-opaque for most
of the fade then snapped to invisible in the final fraction; `.easeOut` ramps
the opacity down steadily from the start so the overlay visibly dissolves
rather than clipping out. Left the 0.3s entrance easeOut, the 1.6s
`asyncAfter` hang time, and the `guard !celebrating` reentrancy guard
untouched, as specified. No changes to `ClearedCelebration.swift` or
`SparkleBurst.swift`.

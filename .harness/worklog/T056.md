# T056: Idle the 60s list ticker while nothing needs it and give it timer tolerance

**Date:** 2026-07-17

## What was done

Implemented energy optimization for the 60-second ticker in `ShoppingListView`:

1. **Tolerance:** Added `tolerance: 6` (10% of 60s interval) to `Timer.publish()` so the OS can coalesce wakeups.
2. **Conditional connection:** 
   - Removed `.autoconnect()` from the ticker and stored the subscription in `@State private var tickerSubscription: Cancellable?`
   - Created `updateTickerConnection()` method that connects the ticker only when `recentlyGot.count > 0` and immediately refreshes `now = AppClock.now` on connect
   - Added `onChange(of: recentlyGot.count)` to monitor when the "Got it" section transitions between empty and non-empty
3. **Pure predicate:** Added `ListLogic.tickerNeeded(_ recentlyGotCount: Int) -> Bool` to express the policy — ticker needed iff recentlyGot is non-empty
4. **Unit test:** Added `testTickerNeededIffRecentlyGotIsNonEmpty` to verify the predicate logic
5. **Documentation:** Updated README.md with trade-off note explaining that with an idle ticker (empty "Got it" section), time-of-day background tint and suggestion recency ranking only refresh on interaction/re-render rather than every 60s

## Trade-off recorded

With ticker idle (empty "Got it" section): time-of-day background tint and suggestion recency ranking only refresh on interaction/re-render rather than every 60s. The tint drifts over hours, not minutes — acceptable, and worth the energy savings. Revisit if a visible seasonality flourish ever needs minute-level refresh on an idle list.
